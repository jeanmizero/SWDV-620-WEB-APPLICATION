Our Menu

1. Mosaic Sushi

Let’s start out with something simply beautiful. Mosaic sushi. Any ingredients work if they’re rolled up right. Making this at home is a bit complicated but the result is pure art. Almost too cool to eat…but not quite

2. Quinoa Sushi

Here’s one for all you trendy vegans out there. Lately quinoa has been all the rage because of its superfood status. So feel extra good about yourself with this hearty and nutrient packed roll

3. Russian Roulette Sushi

Russian Roulette, sushi style, is perfect for those of us who love a good kick. One piece is made super spicy while the rest are made completely normal. Made for sharing, whoever gets the fiery bite is supposed to take the sake shot in the middle

4. Marzipan Sushi

Sushi for dessert?? Yes please. The almond flavor of marzipan paired with sweet rice and mango is not only aesthetically pleasing, but undeniably mouth watering

5. Chili Shrimp Sushi

Some people worry about not feeling full after eating sushi. Worry no more, this chili shrimp roll will do the trick. It’s fully loaded with spicy crab, spicy shrimp, cream cheese, jalapeno, cilantro AND Sriracha

6. Fruit Sushi

Fruit sushi is a super fun DIY and of course, delicious. You can add berries, mango, citrus, banana, kiwi. Seriously, anything you want. Combine your fruity flavors and roll them up in some sweet sticky rice for an adorable treat.
